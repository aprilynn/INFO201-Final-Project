Overview

Here you will find lots of information about colleges across the United States. The data provided includes admission rates, SAT scores, cost of attendance, and diversity. The information we have provided is intended for persons who are looking to attend a university. The data spans from 2006 to 2015 which includes 6842 observations from the different colleges around the US. The average number of colleges observed per year was approximately 685.

Questions

  1. What are the admission rates per university across the nation?
  2. What are the average SAT scores for admitted persons?
  3. What are the cost associated to the universities?
  4. What is the diversity among the universities?
