Overview

Here you will find lots of information about colleges across the United States. The data provided includes admission rates, SAT scores, cost of attendance, and diversity. The information we have provided is intended for persons who are looking to attend a university. The data spans from 2006 to 2015 which includes 7,593 observations from the different colleges around the US. The average number of colleges observed per year is approximately 685.

We have decided to analyze some of the most important factors involved with a decision to attend college. Admission rates give an idea of how many students are accepted out of how many have applied. SAT scores provide the average SAT score for students admitted to the university. Most notably the cost of attendance. Also, we wanted to explore the diversity among the universities.

Questions

  2. What are the average SAT scores for admitted persons?
